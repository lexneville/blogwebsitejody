Unit 1 - 1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects

There are many risks associated with carrying out a project through to completion. These risks include the possibility of key team members departing the company mid-project, loss of data through cyber-attack or theft or inaccurate costings causing a lack of funds to complete the project [5]. We can categorise these risks into 4 types, known/knowns - We are aware of these risks during the planning stage and we have a plan to implement should they occur. known/unknowns - These risks are foreseen but we are not fully prepared to resolve them. unknown/knowns - We are not aware of these risks at the planning stage but we have to ability to resolve them if they occur. unknown/unknowns - We are not aware of these risks and do not know how to respond to them.
