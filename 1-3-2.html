Unit 1 - 3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

To improve my working methods I would plan the project on paper more thoroughly before I started to code or prepare content. I found it easy to become overwhelmed with new ideas and struggled at times to remain focussed on one task at a time. Increasing my use of a kanban board and giving myself tighter deadlines would help me stay on track. In terms of efficiency and productivity of a team, it would be beneficial to split the project into different sub-teams, such as a front-end coding team to work on the visual look of the site or application and a team of back-end coders to work on the functionality of the product. A separate team to prepare the content to be featured in the product would allow for coders to be focussed on the side of the project they are most suited for. In essence we must place people where they will be able to work to their strengths and abilities, this is the key to working effectively as a team.
